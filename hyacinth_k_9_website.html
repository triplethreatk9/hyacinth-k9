Company & Trainer Information

Hyacinth K9 was created to serve dogs and owners who need real-world reliability, clear communication, and honest training. This business was built for people who want a functional dog—not just obedience in a classroom, but calm, confident behavior in everyday life.


Owner & Head Trainer:
Hyacinth K9 is owner-operated and trainer-led. Every dog is trained with intention, structure, and respect. Training is customized to the dog in front of us—not a cookie-cutter system.


We train all breeds, temperaments, and behavior levels. While we do not specialize in bird dogs, we are always willing to evaluate and attempt training based on the individual dog and goals.

Training Packages

Basic Obedience

Foundations for everyday life: sit, down, place, recall, leash manners, and engagement. Ideal for young dogs or owners starting from scratch.

Advanced Obedience

Off-leash reliability, duration, distance, distractions, and real-world proofing. Built for dogs that need control in public and high-distraction environments.

Behavior Modification

Addressing reactivity, aggression, fear, anxiety, and poor impulse control. This package focuses on clarity, boundaries, and confidence-building.

Board & Train

Intensive training where your dog stays with the trainer. Best for owners who want fast, structured results with strong foundations.

Training Philosophy

Hyacinth K9 believes in fair, balanced training built on communication and accountability. Dogs thrive when expectations are clear and leadership is consistent.


Our goal is not perfection—it is reliability. A dog that can exist calmly in the real world is a dog that has been trained correctly.


We do not promise overnight fixes. We promise honest work, clear standards, and results that last.
